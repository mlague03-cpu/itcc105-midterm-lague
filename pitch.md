# ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot - Voice-First Assistant

## Problem Statement
The current ASSCAT portal requires 8-10 clicks to find basic information. Students with low technical literacy struggle to navigate, and the help desk gets overwhelmed during enrollment (100+ tickets daily).

## Industry 5.0 Justification
This AI assistant follows Industry 5.0's "human-centric" approach:
- **Empowers** students with disabilities who cannot navigate complex menus
- **Supports** non-native English speakers through natural language
- **Augments** (not replaces) the registrar staff by handling routine queries

## User Stories (3)

1. As a visually impaired student, I want to speak my requests instead of reading menus, so that I can independently access my student records.

2. As a working student, I want to ask "Do I have pending payments?" so that I can quickly check without logging into multiple systems.

3. As a student during enrollment week, I want the AI to remember my common questions, so that I don't retype the same requests every day.
